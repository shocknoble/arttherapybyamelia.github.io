about

My passion for art started in childhood, as my mother and grandmother both painted what I considered masterpieces. I’ve also always been drawn to the study of people and psychology. When I found out about art therapy in my undergrad, I couldn’t believe that both of the things I loved could be in one profession. I truly found my calling!
